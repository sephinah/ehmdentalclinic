Dental Care Services

Our Clinic Services

Life's fair with EHM Dental Care provides you top of the line dental services and equipment for your dental needs.

Oral Prophylaxis

Procedure done for teeth cleaning. The dentist will remove tartar and plaque build-up from the surfaces of the teeth as well as those hidden in between and under the gums by using electric scaler and hand instrument.

Teeth Extraction / Third Molar Removal

-removal of teeth in dental socket/bone. It is performed if the teeth becomes unrestorable through tooth decay, periodontal disease and dental trauma especially if they are associated with toothache. -third molar removal is done if it is stuck and unable to grow/erupt normally into the mouth and may cause recurrent infections in the gums.

Orthodontics/Braces

Orthodontics is a specialty of dentistry that deals with the diagnosis, prevention and correction of malpositioned teeth and jaws. It can also focus on modifying facial growth, known as dentofacial orthopedics.

Teeth Whitening

Lightens teeth and helps to remove stains and discoloration.

Root Canal Therapy

Root canal treatment used to repair and save the tooth that is badly decayed or becomes infected. .

Teeth Restoration

management of oral health problems, restoring of the mouth to a functional and esthetic state. * teeth fillings * dentures * veneers * crowns and bridges

Clip & Braces

Dental braces (also known as braces, orthodontic cases, or cases) are devices used in orthodontics that align and straighten teeth and help position them with regard to a person's bite, while also aiming to improve dental health. Braces also fix gaps.

Root Canal

A root canal is the naturally occurring anatomic space within the root of a tooth. It consists of the pulp chamber (within the coronal part of the tooth), the main canal(s), and more intricate anatomical branches that may connect the root canals to each other or to the surface of the root.
